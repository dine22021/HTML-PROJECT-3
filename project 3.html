Benifits of Vegetable Salad

Vegetable salad contains Vitamin A, Vitamin C, beta-carotene, calcium, folate, fiber, and phytonutrients.Leafy vegetables are a good choice for a healthful diet because they do not contain cholesterol and are naturally low in calories and sodium.

Steps to make Vegetable Salad

  • Take a large and clear glass bowl
  • Add some chopped fruits and roasted vegetables
  • Add some finely grated vegetables of different texture
  • Add some dry fruits and nuts
  • Add one boiled egg at the top which is a good source of protein
  • Use fresh coriander or chives instead of parsley
